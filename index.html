The BioBots
FRC Team #14318

We are here to ensure that underprivileged students get the opportunity to indulge in First Robotics via our outreach program.

We are the Biobots, a First Tech Challenge (FTC) Team. FTC is an competition organized by FIRST. We did First Lego League (FLL) before transferring to FTC. Every meeting, we have a goal, and we do everything in our power to fulfill that goal until it is fully accomplished. We work hard every single day to achieve our ultimate goal of advancing into the regional finals.
